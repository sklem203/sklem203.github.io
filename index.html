Making things that

I'm a second-year student at Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, pursuing a dual degree in Television, Radio & Film and Data Analytics. I'm drawn to documentary and narrative filmmaking, particularly work that sits at the intersection of culture, history, and human experience.

This summer I'm working with The WWII Foundation in Normandy, France, supporting documentary filmmakers and historians on location. On campus, I serve as Community Service Chair for Kappa Theta Pi, a professional technology fraternity.
